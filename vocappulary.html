VocAPPulary Case Study

What Is VocAPPulary?

VocAPPulary is an app designed to help make studying simple by giving users the ability to create customizable flashcards or search lists of cards that are premade across a variety of categories. My primary role for this project was taking on the role as UX Researcher for the early stages of this app’s development

My Role

  • UX Researcher

Competitive Analysis

I began by completing a competitive analysis to analyze the flashcard and study app market. My research found that while there were other study apps available, none of them contained all of the components that we hoped to create in the VocAPPulary app. Additionally, many of them came with an upgrade cost whereas the business goals of our app required it to be free.

User Interviews

Next, I conducted a series of user-interviews to begin to empathize with the needs of the user and begin to understand their needs and goals while using our app. These interviews revealed that studying new topics can be overwhelming for users because the amount of new information can be vast. Their needs revealed having searchable categories and premade lists available would help them get started, and it was important to them to have the flexibility to add their own study cards as they learned more.

User Personas

Next came the process of creating a user persona for the target audience of our VocAPPulary app.

User Flow & Task Analysis

Following our personas came creating a task analysis which then provided insight into the creation of user flows. This user-flow demonstrates the ability for the user to search the word or phrase in another language for translation, and then to save the translation as a study card.

Wireframing

From these user-flows, I was able to create low-fidelity wire frames for various aspects of the app.

Design Handoff

With my responsibilities of conducting all of the initial user research completed, at this point I handed off my wireframe recommendations to the VocAPPulary UI Design and Development team
